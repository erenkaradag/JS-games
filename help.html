The story

you are a spaceman, who was supposed to go to the Moon. However, as you were leaving Earth, your spaceship failed, with you falling back into Earth.

Tutorial

Your objective is to bounce with your spaceship, collecting as much coins as you can before you run out of boost and speed

You can rotate your ship to get the best possible speed

The high score is the key in Stellar, you must travel far and wide into the horizon to build up your high score. Compare and compete with your friends!

Get to know space!

Stellar is a game that was built for raising children's awareness for the efforts put into space exploration, in hopes that a new generation of children would be inspired to learn more about space.

I was inspired to make this game for you, mainly inspired from NASA's journey to expand their travels further than before

Apollo missions were various objectives, all set towards focusing on our moon. Apollo 11 was the first spacecraft to land on the moon. This inspired me to make Stellar for you.
